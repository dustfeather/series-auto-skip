# Privacy Policy

**Auto Skip for Plex & Netflix** does not collect, store, or transmit any user data.

## What the extension does

The extension runs a content script on Netflix and Plex pages that watches for skip buttons and clicks them automatically. All processing happens locally in your browser.

## What the extension does not do

- Does not collect personal information
- Does not track browsing activity
- Does not make network requests
- Does not use cookies or local storage
- Does not use analytics or telemetry
- Does not share any data with third parties

## Permissions

The extension requests access to Netflix and Plex URLs solely to inject the content script that automates button clicks. No other permissions are requested or used.

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, open an issue on [GitHub](https://github.com/dustfeather/series-auto-skip/issues) or email dustfeather@gmail.com.
